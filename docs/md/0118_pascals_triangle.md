## 118. Pascal's Triangle

-  [LeetCode](https://leetcode.com/problems/pascals-triangle/) | [LeetCode CH](https://leetcode.cn/problems/pascals-triangle/) (Easy)

-   Generate the first `numRows` of Pascal's triangle.

```plaintext
                 numRows    index
     1              1         0
    1 1             2         1
   1 2 1            3         2
  1 3 3 1           4         3
 1 4 6 4 1          5         4
```
